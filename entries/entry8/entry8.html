ENTRY 8

My daily ritual of exercise or movement, whether it’s a gym session, a stretch, or a walk, serves as a dedicated time to reconnect with my body and mind, grounding myself amid daily tasks. Each activity, even a brief walk, plays a role in building a sense of balance and well-being. Physical activity supports this balance by engaging both body and mind, offering a moment to destress, clear my head, and improve my mood through endorphins. For example, a gym workout challenges my physical limits, strengthening resilience, while stretching or walking offers a gentler approach, releasing tension and promoting calm. These movement routines don't just keep me physically active; they’re part of a self-care system, helping me stay energized, centered, and ready to approach my day with a fresh outlook. This ritual underscores the importance of movement as a tool for maintaining harmony, reminding me to stay present and aligned with my personal wellness goals.

WHAT IS THIS?

A fun, goofy game to help you get started working out or spice up your workout routine.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Click the button to spin the wheel. Whatever exercise you get, click the Start timer button to run the 45-second timer. When the time is up, click the Spin again to get back to the spin wheel. Do as many times as you like. This means if you get burpee seven times in a row, do it anyway.

WHY DID I MAKE IT?

Getting started is always the hardest part of cultivating a new habit. We could all use a little help with gamification.

WHAT IS IT NOT?

This is not about getting a six-pack abs in six weeks. Sometimes, however, doing something is still better than nothing at all. Enjoy
