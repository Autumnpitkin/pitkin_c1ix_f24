ENTRY 7

My daily ritual of listening to my favorite true crime podcast is a cherished blend of suspense and comfort, adding excitement and mental engagement to my day. I have a dedicated time or setting for this activity, whether it's during a commute while relaxing at home, or accompanying daily tasks. As the podcast begins, I am drawn into each case, captivated by the host’s storytelling and the unfolding mystery. The narratives spark my curiosity, making me feel as though I am part of the investigation, eagerly anticipating each new episode or plot twist. After listening, I often find myself reflecting on the details, analyzing the facts, and contemplating the psychology behind the stories, adding a layer of intrigue to my routine. This ritual is both an escape and a stimulating break from daily life, offering familiarity with the podcast’s style and host, along with the thrill of each new case.


I truly admire this podcast/channel. Annie Elise is the creator behind the popular YouTube channel 10toLife and the podcast Serialously where she explores various true crime stories. She is known for her honest approach and for combining thorough research with engaging storytelling, which makes every episode both informative and captivating. Annie Elise emphasizes the importance of giving victims a voice and telling their stories to seek justice and bring about change in broken systems, unjust court decisions, and outdated laws. Although some of the stories and images she presents can be difficult to process, she believes we owe it to the victims to listen and acknowledge the atrocities they faced. It is through this discomfort and heartbreak that listeners are inspired to foster a deep desire for change, motivating us to strive for a better and safer world.
