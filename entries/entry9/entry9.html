COOKING

Ritual

ENTRY 9



My cooking ritual is a beautifully orchestrated process that offers comfort and grounding in its simplicity and mindfulness. It begins with the deliberate selection of ingredients—each chosen with care, reflecting my intuition and preferences for that moment. This step alone establishes a connection between myself and the materials of your meal, setting the tone for creativity and nurture.

As I move to preparation, the rhythm of chopping, stirring, and seasoning becomes almost meditative. The sensory engagement—the sizzle of oil, the aroma of fresh herbs, and the vibrant colors of ingredients—grounds me in the present. This stage transforms cooking into a tactile and immersive experience, turning the kitchen into a sanctuary of focus and expression. Finally, as the meal comes together and I savor the first bite, there’s a profound sense of fulfillment—not just from the flavors but from the act of having created something nourishing and personal. This moment closes the ritual, providing a sense of completeness, calm, and joy that lingers long after the meal is over.

It’s more than cooking; it’s a practice of care, mindfulness, and creative fulfillment.

TACO RECIPE/STEPS:

1. Make Pico de Gallo

2. Prepare Sour Cream

3. Cook Ground Beef

4. Warm Corn Tortillas

5. Assemble Tacos

6. Enjoy!

Discover the Art of Tacos: A Step-by-Step Guide


  1. Make Pico de Gallo:

    Dice fresh tomatoes, red onions, jalapeños, and cilantro.

    Add lime juice and salt. (Recipe below for detailed steps.)

  2. Prepare Sour Cream:

    Combine Greek yogurt, lime/lemon juice, and salt.

    Let it rest for flavors to meld. (Detailed recipe below.)

  3. Cook Ground Beef:

    Heat a pan, add a drizzle of oil, and cook the ground beef until browned.

    Stir in your favorite taco seasoning blend, adding a splash of water for saucier consistency. Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes.

  4. Warm Corn Tortillas:

    Toast corn tortillas directly on a stove burner or in a dry pan for a smoky flavor.

  5. Assemble Tacos:

    Layer beef, a spoonful of Pico de Gallo, sour cream, shredded Mexican cheese, and a squeeze of lime juice.

    Add optional toppings like avocado slices, pickled red onions, or hot sauce.

  6. Enjoy!

Pico de Gallo Recipe:

Ingredients:

2 ripe Roma tomatoes, diced small
1 tablespoon finely diced red onion
½ tablespoon diced jalapeños (adjust for heat preference)
1 teaspoon lime juice
1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
Pinch of Himalayan pink salt or kosher salt

Steps:

1. Dice all the ingredients finely for a cohesive texture.
2. Mix them in a bowl until well combined.
3. Taste and adjust lime juice or salt as needed. Let it sit for 10 minutes for flavors to meld.


Sour Cream Recipe:

Ingredients:

½ cup Greek yogurt (plain, unsweetened)
1 tablespoon lime juice (adjust to taste)
1 teaspoon Himalayan pink salt

Steps:

1. Whisk the yogurt with lime juice and salt in a small bowl.
2. Taste and adjust lime juice or salt as needed.
3. Cover and let it rest for at least 5 minutes.
This step enhances the tangy flavor.
4. Refrigerate if making ahead.

This recipe celebrates the comforting ritual of cooking while enhancing flavor and love into every bite! ENJOY 🌮
